Minahil Raza

Doctoral Researcher at Aalto University

I am a Doctoral Researcher in the Aalto Robot Learning Group at Aalto University, supervised by Prof. Joni Pajarinen. My research focuses on robot learning, particularly reinforcement learning for robot manipulation and autonomous excavation, with an emphasis on online adaptation of learning algorithms in real-world robotic systems.

Previously, I completed an Erasmus Mundus Joint Master's Degree (EMJMD) in Engineering of Data-intensive Intelligent Software Systems (EDISS), a joint programme consisting of:

  • Computer Engineering — Åbo Akademi University
  • Intelligent Systems — University of the Balearic Islands
Prior to starting my doctoral studies, I was working on autonomous load handling for heavy machines at Hiab, where I filed two patents detailing novel methods for load handling and user interfaces for heavy machinery. During my master's studies, I have been a part of the research team at Nokia Bell Labs, where I completed my master's thesis titled Whole-body Manipulation using Reinforcement Learning (available at Doria).
